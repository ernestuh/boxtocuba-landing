Shipping rates to La Habana

Prices in Canadian dollars (CAD). Rate is based on whichever is greater: actual weight or volumetric weight.

Weight (lbs) Rate Example total Notes
0.5 – 10 lbs $75.00 CAD flat $75.00 Fixed price regardless of weight
10.01 – 44 lbs $6.50 CAD/lb 20 lbs → $130.00 Per pound rate
44.01+ lbs $6.00 CAD/lb 50 lbs → $300.00 Best rate for heavy boxes
Volumetric weight formula: Length × Width × Height (cm) ÷ 139 = volumetric kg. We charge the greater of actual vs. volumetric. Pack compactly to optimize cost.

* Rate includes air freight and delivery in Cuba (door-to-door or pickup point depending on municipality). Canada pickup available in GTA only for a fee. Outside GTA: ship via courier to our Toronto location.

FAQ

Questions about shipping to La Habana

How long does it take to ship a package to La Habana? +

Delivery to La Habana typically takes 7–12 business days from pickup. La Habana has the most frequent flight schedule — packages are prioritized on the next available flight after processing.

Do you deliver door-to-door in La Habana? +

Delivery options depend on the specific municipality within La Habana. For most areas we offer door-to-door delivery; for some we use a nearby pickup point. Our team will confirm the delivery method at the time of booking.

What is the cheapest way to ship to La Habana from Canada? +

La Habana has our lowest starting rate — from $6/lb CAD — and benefits from the most frequent flight schedule, which means faster processing. The most cost-effective approach is to maximize the weight per box: packing densely reduces volumetric weight charges.

Can I send medicine to La Habana? +

Yes. Medications in original packaging, up to USD $200 in value, are exempt from Cuban customs duty. Please do not remove medications from their original containers. Contact us if you're unsure about a specific medication.
